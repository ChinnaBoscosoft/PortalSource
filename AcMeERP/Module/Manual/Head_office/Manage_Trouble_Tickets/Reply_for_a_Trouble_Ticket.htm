Manage Trouble Tickets

Reply for a Trouble Ticket

The trouble tickets posted by the Head office users can be viewed by the Head office admin. The head office admin or users  can give solutions for the trouble tickets by replying for the trouble tickets.

To reply for a trouble ticket

  1. Click Ticket > Post Ticket.
    The Ticket View page is displayed. View image

     

  2. Click on the tickets in the Ticket View page.
    The Reply Ticket dialog box is displayed. View image

  3. Enter description in the Description box.

  4. Click Save.
    The Successfully saved message is displayed.

  5. Click Close to go back to Tickets view page.
