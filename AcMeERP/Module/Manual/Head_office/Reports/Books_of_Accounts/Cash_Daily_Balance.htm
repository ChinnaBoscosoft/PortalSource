Books of Accounts

Cash Daily balance

Cash daily balance shows the details of daily cash inflow and outflow for a project for a specified date range.

To generate cash daily balance:

  1. Click Reports > Books of Accounts > Cash Daily Balance.
    The Cash Daily Balance customise dialog box is displayed. View image

  2. Select the options in the customise dialog box.

  3. Click Ok.
    The     Cash Daily Balance report is displayed. View image
