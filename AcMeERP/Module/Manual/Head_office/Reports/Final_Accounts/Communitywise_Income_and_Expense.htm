Communitywise Income and Expenditure

Communitywise income and expenditure report shows the list of the incomes and expenditures of all the branch offices.

To generate communitywise income and expenditure:

  1. Click Reports > Final Accounts > Communitywise Income And Expenditure.
    The Communitywise Income and Expenditure customise dialog box is displayed. View image

  2. Select the options in the customise dialog box.

  3. Click Ok.
    The Communitywise Income and Expenditure report is displayed. View image
