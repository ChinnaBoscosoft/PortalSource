Vouchers

View Vouchers

The vouchers uploaded by the branch office can be viewed in the voucher page.

To view voucher:

  • Click Data > Vouchers > Vouchers.
    The Vouchers page is displayed. View Image

  • To view vouchers of another branch: Select Branch, Date From, Date To, Vouchers (Receipt, Paymenwt, Contra and Journal), Project and click Go.
