Final Accounts

Income and Expenditure

Income and expenditure report shows the list of all the Incomes and Expenditures. It considers only revenue items and no Capital transactions are included.

To generate income and expenditure:

  1. Click Reports > Final Accounts > Income And Expenditure.
    The Income and expenditure customise dialog box is displayed. View image  

  2. Select the options in the customise dialog box.

  3. Click Ok.
    The Income and Expenditure report is displayed. View image
