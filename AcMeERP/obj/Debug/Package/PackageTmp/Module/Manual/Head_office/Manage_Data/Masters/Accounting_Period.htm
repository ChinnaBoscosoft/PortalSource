Accounting period

Accounting Period is the time span during which financial events took place and recorded in the organization. It is defined in the head office.

To add accounting Period:

  1. Click Data > Masters > Accounting Period.
    The Accounting Period page is displayed. View image

  2. Click Create Accounting Period in the upper-right side of the Accounting Period page.
    The Accounting Period Add page is displayed. View image

  3. Select the Year to date from the calendar box.

  4. Click Save.
    The Saved Successfully message is displayed.

  5. Click Ok.

  6. Click Close to go back to Accounting Period view page.

 

  • The Year From date in the Accounting Period Add page is selected automatically based on the previous accounting period's Year To date. The next day of the pervious accounting period's Year To date is shown in the Year From calender box.   

  • All the accounting periods can't be inactive, one accounting period must be active always and more than one accounting period cannot be active.
