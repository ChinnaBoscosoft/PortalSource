Legal Entity

A legal entity is a registered unit of persons or organisations to act as if they were a single social unit for certain purposes. They form a society and are managed by their heads at different levels. Each project in the branch office comes under any one of legal entity.

    To add legal entity:

    1. Click Data > Masters > Legal Entity.
      The Legal Entity View page is displayed. View image

    2. Click Add Legal Entity in the upper-right side of the Legal Entity view page. View image  

    3. Type Society Name, Contact Person, Address, Place, and Phone in the boxes.

    4. Select Country from the list.

    5. Type State/Province, Pincode, Fax, Email and URL in the boxes.

    6. Enter Society/Reg.No in the box.

    7. Select Reg.Date from the calender box.

    8. Enter Prior Permission No in the box.

    9. Select Prior Permission Date from the calendar box.

    10. Type 12A No, PAN No, GIR No, TAN No, and FCRA No in the boxes.  

    11. Select FCRA Reg.Date from the calender box.

    12. Enter 80G.NO in the box.

    13. Select Association Nature by selecting the appropriate check box.

    14. Select Denomination by clicking on the appropriate option.

    15. Click Save.
      The Legal Entity is Saved message is displayed.

    16. Click Close to go back to Legal Entity view page.
