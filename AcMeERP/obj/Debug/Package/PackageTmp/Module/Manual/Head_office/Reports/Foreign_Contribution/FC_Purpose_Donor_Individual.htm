Foreign Contribution

FC Purpose Donor Individual  

FC purpose donor individual report shows the foreign contribution details received from an individual and the grand total of all the contributions.

To generate FC Purpose Donor Individual:

  1. Click Reports > Foreign Contribution > FC Purpose Donor Individual.
    The FC Purpose Donor Individual customise dialog box is displayed.  View image

  2. Select the options in the customise dialog box.

  3. Click OK.
    The FC Purpose Donor Individual report is displayed. View image
